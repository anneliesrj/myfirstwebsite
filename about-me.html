About me

Hi! I'm Annelies. Thank you for visiting my website.
Let me tell you a little bit about myself. I was born in the Netherlands and have lived there most of my life. In late 2019 I moved to the UK to move in with my partner.

Right before the COVID outbreak, I found a job in education in a support role. I knew this was not the long-term job for me, but I was happy to have a job and income during the difficult 2 years. Last year I followed a course in Counselling and was thinking about continuing further education in Counselling. When I was accepted on a university course, I started doubting this decision and was thinking back about the other thing I always thought I might like and would be good at; coding.

And here I am! Writing code and creating websites. 2 months in now, and I am very happy with this decision. Hopefully this time next year, I will have a new job in software development/engineering.

Background

At university I have studied Communication & Marketing, where I specialised in creative communication. During these years I have done a lot of designing and writing. Designing was one of my favourite parts.

After University I have worked in marketing and branding for a big Dutch retailer where I have done several projects. After a year and a half, I left to travel for 5 months. When I came back, I started working in a college in several different roles.
